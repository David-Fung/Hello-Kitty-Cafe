About

At the Hello Kitty Cafe, we offer a large variety of delicious Hello Kitty themed treats and drinks. We also offer breakfast, lunch and dinner hours. Stop by the Hello Kitty Cafe at Irvine, California and try out our adorable treats!
Our Story

Hello Kitty Cafe first began its journey with the Hello Kitty Truck in 2014. This success led to the first-of-its-kind Hello Kitty Cafe Pop-Up Container in 2016 and the Hello Kitty Mini Cafe set within malls or lifestyle centers across California in 2017 both boasting an expanded menu of supercute treats and drinks. Today, fans can enjoy the Hello Kitty Cafe experience nationally with more to come in the future.
